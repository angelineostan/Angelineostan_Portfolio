HCI 102 Project

QUICKTRIKE

Overview

QuickTrike is a high-fidelity UI/UX prototype designed to modernize local transportation. It addresses the difficulty of hailing tricycles in busy areas by connecting passengers and drivers through a seamless, mobile-first interface optimized for local transit needs.

The Design Challenge

The primary challenge was designing for a diverse user base. I had to ensure the interface was intuitive enough for tricycle drivers who may have limited tech experience, while maintaining a modern, efficient booking flow for students and daily commuters.

Key UX Features

  • ▹ One-Tap Booking Simplified location selection to minimize cognitive load.
  • ▹ Dual Dashboards Distinct, high-contrast interfaces for both Passenger and Driver modes.
  • ▹ Transparent Fare System Real-time distance-based pricing to ensure fair transactions.
